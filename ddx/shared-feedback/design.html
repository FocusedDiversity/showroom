Visual

  • Own comments: gray background (`bg-gray-50`), labeled "You"
  • Others' comments: white background with a left accent border in arctic blue (#A1B8CA)
  • Author line: bold obfuscated email or "You", followed by · and relative timestamp
  • Comments are interleaved chronologically — not separated into own/other sections

Interaction

  • All feedback for this slide loads when the panel opens or slide changes
  • New submissions from the current viewer appear immediately in the list
  • Others' new comments appear on next load (no real-time push)
